The Annual College Fest "TechnoNova" is the flagship cultural and technical event of the institute, usually held in mid-February. It serves as a platform for students to showcase their talents outside the classroom.

Key highlights of the fest include:
- Hackathons: 24-hour coding marathons where students build solutions for real-world problems.
- Cultural Night: Dance, music, and drama performances featuring student clubs and guest artists.
- Robotics Expo: A showcase of student-built robots competing in obstacle courses and "robo-wars."
- Gaming Zone: Competitive e-sports tournaments for popular games like Valorant and FIFA.

The fest attracts participation from over 50 colleges across the state and promotes networking, leadership, and teamwork among the student body.
